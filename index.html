Training Programs

WE TRAIN YOUR PEOPLE,
NOT JUST EQUIP THEM

Hands-on fire safety training conducted at your premises — building real preparedness, not just compliance checkboxes.

Program 01

Basic Fire Safety Training

Practical training on fire behavior, extinguisher operation (PASS technique), and initial response with live fire demonstrations.

Program 02

Basic First Aid Training

CPR and AED training, wound management, burn treatment, and emergency patient handling with mannequins and real-world simulation.

Program 03

Advance Fire Safety — Hydrant

Hydrant system operation for ERT teams — hose handling, pressure control, team coordination and live deployment exercises.

Program 04

Evacuation Drill Training

Structured mock drills for entire organizations — assembly points, warden responsibilities, disabled evacuation, and post-drill reports.

About Us

BUILT ON TRUST,
BACKED BY EXPERTISE

Sri Sai Fire Safety Equipments is a Ranipet-based specialized dealer and installer of industrial fire safety and personal protective equipment. We partner with India's top manufacturers and deliver their best products directly to your facility — with the expertise to install, maintain, and train around them.

We serve a wide range of clients — from fast-scaling tech campuses and manufacturing plants to schools, hospitals, and new construction projects. Our 72+ client roster spans industries from automotive and pharma to education and retail.

"Serve to Save Life & Property" — our guiding principle across every product we supply and every system we install.
